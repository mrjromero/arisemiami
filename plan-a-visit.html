Getting to know each other

We know meeting someone for the first time can be intimidating, and going to a new church for the first time can make you nervous. We want to help make your first experience at ARISE is a good one!

SERVICE TIMES

ARISE meets every weekend on Saturdays at 11:15 a.m. at 14266 SW 119th Ave, Miami, FL 33186.

WHAT CAN I EXPECT?

How long is an ARISE service?

In total, an ARISE service is about 90 minutes in length. Services begin with the ARISE worship band leading the church in music - song lyrics are projected on screens so you can sing along and/or engage with worship however you feel most comfortable. After the music portion is complete, one of our pastors will come out to share an encouraging and hope-filled message about Jesus. 

What's the culture like at ARISE?

Saturdays at ARISE are exciting, casual, and relaxed. Come as you are and expect to feel welcomed as our guest.

What about my kids?

We believe that kids should have a blast at ARISE every single week and we make this a priority. The other thing we make a priority is your children's safety. Because of that, we have a detailed check-in process for our ARISE Kids program the first time you visit. The kids experience is for ages 3 to 10. You'll want to leave yourself an extra ten minutes to get signed in for the ARISE Kids experience. ARISE Kids experience begin at 11:15 A.M. in parallel with the ARISE Worship Service.
